At age 18, Thuso embarked on a journey to the local university where he sought to learn all the skills he would need to keep his vow. He studied Astrophysics, Computer Science, Math, Physics, and learned from renowned PI's and Post-docs, mastering every programming style. In time, Thuso forged himself into a living weapon to wage war on unsolved Astrophysics problems. Upon his return to Cape Town, Thuso stalked astrophysics problems as a plainclothes Ph.D student. Frozen by the beauty of the night sky, he barely survived his first night out observing. As he sat shivering in the observatory Thuso knew that he had to first strike fear in the hearts of his Astrophysical problems. Just then, a meteor crashed through the observatory window, giving Thuso the inspiration he needed.

My research interests include computational astronomy, transient phenomena, the precise measurement of atmosphere for astronomical observations, diffuse HI and etc..
